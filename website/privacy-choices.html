Privacy Choices

Last updated: 17 April 2026

At GlossyQuote, we believe you should have control over your personal data. This page explains the choices available to you and how to exercise them.

Your Privacy Options

📤 Export Your Data

Download a copy of all personal data we hold about you, including your profile, estimates, and activity history. Exported as a JSON file.

In-app: Profile → Export My Data

🗑️ Delete Your Account

Close your account and request deletion of your personal data. We will delete your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes (e.g. transaction records for 7 years).

In-app: Profile → Close Account

🔔 Push Notifications

Control which notifications you receive — new leads, messages, and account alerts. You can turn off all notifications or customise by type.

In-app: Settings → Notifications

📍 Location Data

We use your approximate location (postcode/region) to show relevant local tradespeople and leads. You can deny or revoke location access at any time from your device settings without losing access to the app.

Device: Settings → GlossyQuote → Location

✏️ Correct Your Data

Update your name, email, phone number, or other profile details directly within the app, or contact us and we will correct any inaccurate information.

In-app: Profile → Edit Profile

🚫 Object to Processing

You have the right to object to certain types of data processing, including processing based on our legitimate interests. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.

📧 glossyquote@gmail.com

📵 Marketing Communications

If you have opted in to marketing emails or SMS messages, you can opt out at any time by clicking "Unsubscribe" in any marketing email or by contacting us directly.

Contact us to opt out

Cookies

Our mobile app does not use cookies. Our website (glossyquote.com) only uses essential cookies required for basic functionality such as navigation. We do not currently use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies.

If this changes in the future, we will update this page and ask for your consent where required.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising. This section is provided for compliance with applicable US state privacy laws (e.g. California CCPA) where applicable to our users.
